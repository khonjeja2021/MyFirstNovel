What makes a team?
Katzenbach and Smith point out that work groups are not necessarily synonymous with teams, 
and that it is important to recognize 
the distinction between them so you know whether or not making the effort to generate a 
“team dynamic” will actually yield better outcomes. 
Specifically, they define a team as, "a small number of people with complementary skills 
who are committed to a common purpose, set of performance goals, and approach for which 
they hold themselves mutually accountable.”
